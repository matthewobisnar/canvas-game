Instructions
  • A pattern of colorful pieces will appear on the right side of the screen.
  • You will have to recreate such pattern on the left side.
  • Choose from the pieces at the bottom of the screen to recreate and match the pattern shown. Unless, you are asked to rotate to a specific degree otherwise.
  • Drag the pieces to the empty grid on the right side to recreate the pattern on the left.
  • Pieces that are placed poorly or overlap will be reset.
  • Be patient! Sometimes, pieces will have to be rotated many times to fit perfectly! Click the box to rotate.
  • Now, try dragging one piece to the grid on the left. Then tap to rotate the piece so it matches the target pattern.
