Instructions
  • This exercise gives you various selections that require proofreading.
  • Your task is to identify the errors – misspells, typos, grammar and punctuation, that have been strewn throughout the selection.
  • Identify an error in the selection below.
  • There is error counter on the right side of the screen. Make sure to refer how many errors are still left.
  • When you select a word, punctuation or grammar that is NOT an error, it will result in an incorrect mark. Three incorrect marks will result to a default and you’ll have to move to the next selection.
  • Longer selections will require you to scroll down the page to be able to read the entire document.
